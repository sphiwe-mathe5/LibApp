Meet The Managing Director

Sibusiso Zulu : Managing Director

Sibusiso is the visionary Founding Member and Managing Director of Libubu Mining Group Pty Ltd. With a solid educational foundation, Sibusiso holds a National Diploma in Industrial Engineering from the prestigious University of Johannesburg.

Under Sibusiso's leadership, Libubu Mining Group has grown into a respected player in the mining industry, recognized for its cutting-edge practices and dedication to community development. His strategic vision and hands-on approach have been instrumental in navigating the company through challenges and driving continuous improvement.

Sibusiso's journey is a testament to the power of hard work, dedication, and a clear vision. His leadership continues to propel Libubu Mining Group towards new heights, making a lasting impact on the industry and the communities it serves.
